The Boat Job

Description: Farlight has contacted Mingus about a job. The main baron of Craydon, Baron Bluhd, is heading back to his mansion on Craydon from Aston. He is transporting his black book of blackmail and secrets that allow him to stay in power. A anonymous source has hired the Current to steal the book for 25,000 gold. They have also offered an additional 25,000 gold to do this while also replacing Baron Bluhd with a look a like to prevent others from trying to fill the void of power that would be left.
